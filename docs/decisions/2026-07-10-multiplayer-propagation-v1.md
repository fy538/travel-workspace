# Decision: V1 multiplayer propagation and authority

## Context

Group-visible mutations had inconsistent delivery, attribution, privacy, and audit
behavior. A 2026-07-09 matrix produced nine accepted founder rulings.

## Decision

V1 uses tally-plus-own-vote privacy; existing push classes plus refetch-on-focus;
wiki-mode plans; quiet group-visible direct edits; organizer resolution with limited
unanimous auto-accept and no timeout; fixed organizer authority; group-visible
expenses except payer-covered masking; dignity-preserving membership fan-out; and
member-operable room mute with group/audit evidence.

## Consequences

No new realtime channel, selective draft layer, fluid authority model, or per-vote
push is required for cohort one. System charters and second-observer tests own
conformance; the investigation matrix becomes historical evidence.

## Revisit trigger

Observed cohort confusion, coordination harm, or privacy discomfort that cannot be
fixed within these rules.
